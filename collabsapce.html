Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CollabSpace

Is CollabSpace free to use?▼

Yes! CollabSpace is completely free for all students. Just register with your college email and start collaborating!

How do I join a project?▼

Go to the Projects page, find a project you like, and click the Join button. You will be added instantly!

Can I create my own study group?▼

Absolutely! Go to Study Groups page and click Create Study Group. Set your course, description and invite members.

How do contribution points work?▼

You earn points by creating projects (+50), joining groups (+10), uploading resources (+20), and commenting (+5). Higher points = higher leaderboard rank!

Is my data secure?▼

Yes! We use JWT authentication, bcrypt password hashing, and rate limiting to keep your data safe.
